About Me

My personal mantra:

To design conversations and solutions that appeal to the heart of people and keep them engaged in a way that compliments the brand.

Why UX/UI?

I began my career as a UX/UI designer after several years as an electrical engineering consultant. UX/UI gave me a way to build on my skills in project management, collaboration, and meticulousness with detail to learn how to design creatively.

What has influenced me?

I’ve lived in and traveled to 15 countries including Japan, Austria, and Australia. But no matter where I go I always make new friends and create space in what began as unfamiliar. In my spare time I enjoy silversmithing, guitar, Anime, sculpting running marathons, and working with my nonprofit, Heal Georgia.
